My Story

I'm a Frontend Developer with a background in non-profit and campaign work. I made the career switch into software development because of my love of problem solving. I like to be challenged by problems and create clever solutions to solve them. There is very little more exciting than building out an idea into a application.

I'm a graduate of Grand Circus' Frontend Bootcamp. I was hooked on programming from the very first time I ran a line of my code, and saw the result on the screen. I'm interested in working on ambitious projects with positive solution-oriented people.

When I'm not writing code, I'm on the hunt for an incredible cup of coffee, enjoying a great soccer game, or challenging myself by learning a completely new skill. I can also finish almost any quote from the 1984 classic, Ghostbusters.

Born and raised in Pennsylvania, went to school in Ohio, now living in the beautiful Michigan.
